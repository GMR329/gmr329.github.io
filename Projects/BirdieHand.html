Birdie Hand

Flipping Birdie

The Project:

While going through old junk from college, I found the scraps from this gizmo. This was one of my earlier (and perhaps less classy) simple robotics projects. It uses a DC motor for pivoting and a stepper motor for actuating 4/5 of the fingers. I was strangely proud of this one, and I hope to carry the larger theme into future robotics projects: robots need a bit of backbone. And, maybe a little more tension in the strings to really get the point across. I mean, how hard would that have been?
