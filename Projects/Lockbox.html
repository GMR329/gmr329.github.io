The Project:

This simple lockbox was created before I got my 3D printer. It was great integrating wood working with this fun build. This box opens a door on a DC motor only if the correct password is typed and closes after hitting ‘C’ followed by ‘#’. While it has not been thoroughly rated by lock-safe professionals, I would say it’s probably fire proof so long as it does not come into contact with fire, and can withstand the impact of one Boeing 737 door fallen from a maximum of 8,266 feet.

At a Glance:

  • Used Arduino to take input from keypad
  • Used DC motor and driver board to open and close door
